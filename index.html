Read-Only Personal Finance

FuelD

FuelD helps people understand balances, spending, cash flow, liabilities, and financial progress over time. The app is designed to be read-only: it analyzes data, explains changes clearly, and does not move money.

Privacy Policy Plaid Return Page

Read-only by design

FuelD is built to help users review balances, spending, and obligations without initiating transfers, payments, or bill pay actions.

Connected through Plaid

Users can connect bank accounts, credit cards, and loans through Plaid so FuelD can display balances, transactions, and liabilities in one place.

Focused on trust

FuelD uses authentication, explicit user consent, and controlled data access to keep the experience transparent and grounded in verified account data.
